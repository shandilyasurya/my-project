What it Does:
1.Displays a balance.
2.Shows two action buttons: Deposit and Withdraw.
3.Works on both desktop and mobile devices.

HTML Overview:

1.Uses a main container to center the content.
2.Displays balance in bold, green text.
3.Has two buttons inside a flex container.

CSS Overview:

1.Uses Flexbox to center content and align items.
2.Adds padding, border, background color, and spacing.
3.Green and red colors for the buttons.
Uses a media query to make layout vertical on small screens (mobile-friendly).
